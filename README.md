# Suno 音乐训练器（Suno Music Trainer）

一个面向 Suno 用户的音乐英文学习网页。它把 Suno 常见英文、音乐术语、提示词写法、歌词结构和歌曲制作流程做成小游戏和模拟训练，让用户在玩闯关游戏的过程中熟悉 Suno 的英文界面，并逐步学会如何描述和生成自己想要的歌曲。

A music English learning web app for Suno users. It turns common Suno English terms, music vocabulary, prompt writing, lyric structure, and song creation workflows into mini-games and simulations, helping users learn Suno’s English interface and practice generating the songs they want.

## 项目亮点 Features

### 中文

- 词汇闯关：通过单词怪学习 Suno 和音乐制作常见英文。
- 听力练习：朗读英文词汇，帮助用户记住发音和含义。
- 提示词工坊：用模块化方式组合风格、情绪、人声、节奏、旋律、音色和结构，生成可用于 Suno 的英文提示词。
- 乐理地图：从歌曲结构、节奏、旋律、和声、编曲到完整表达，循序渐进学习基础乐理。
- 制作流程训练：模拟从写需求、选风格、写歌词、生成歌曲、挑选版本到延长歌曲的完整流程。
- 模拟歌词编辑器：练习 `[Verse]`、`[Chorus]`、`[Bridge]`、`[Outro]` 等 Suno 常用歌词结构。
- Suno 模拟工作台：模拟 Create、Lyrics、Style、Upload、Extend、Library 等常见功能入口。

### English

- Vocabulary battles: Learn common Suno and music production English terms through word challenges.
- Listening practice: Hear English terms aloud to remember pronunciation and meaning.
- Prompt workshop: Combine genre, mood, vocals, rhythm, melody, timbre, and structure to generate usable Suno prompts.
- Music theory map: Learn basic music theory step by step, from song structure and rhythm to melody, harmony, arrangement, and full prompt expression.
- Creation flow training: Practice the full workflow from idea writing, style selection, lyric writing, song generation, version selection, and song extension.
- Mock lyrics editor: Practice Suno-friendly lyric sections such as `[Verse]`, `[Chorus]`, `[Bridge]`, and `[Outro]`.
- Suno simulator: Learn common functional areas such as Create, Lyrics, Style, Upload, Extend, and Library.

## 适合谁使用 Who Is It For

### 中文

这个项目适合正在使用 Suno 但不熟悉英文界面、音乐术语或提示词写法的用户。它也适合想学习基础乐理、歌词结构和 AI 音乐创作流程的人。

### English

This project is designed for Suno users who are not familiar with English interfaces, music terms, or prompt writing. It is also useful for anyone who wants to learn basic music theory, lyric structure, and AI music creation workflows.
